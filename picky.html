Picky is a judgemental trashcan robot that gives you a side-eye when you don’t recycle properly. By raising its eyebrows in approval for correct recycling, Picky encourages playful and responsible recycling habits.

This project was created at Imperial College London (10.2024-11.2024) with Nefeli Vitoraki and Xiangnan Wu. My role included concept, interaction design, and machine learning through Wekinator.
